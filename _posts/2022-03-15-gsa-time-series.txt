There are many tools to perform gene set analysis on time-series data. I have reviewed seven of them and here I share some quick tutorials that I have written as interactive jupyter notebooks.

## Jupyter tutorials:
The tutorials can be found here:

1. [STEM](https://github.com/mora-lab/benchmarks/blob/master/time-course/workflows/STEM/STEM.ipynb)

2. [GSEA for time series](https://github.com/mora-lab/benchmarks/blob/master/time-course/workflows/GSEA_for_time_series/GSEA_for_time_series.ipynb)

3. [SEA --maSigFun, PCA-maSigFun, and ASCA-functional--](https://github.com/mora-lab/benchmarks/blob/master/time-course/workflows/SEA/SEA.ipynb)

4. [timeClip](https://github.com/mora-lab/benchmarks/blob/master/time-course/workflows/TimeClip/TimeClip.ipynb)

5. [TcGSA](https://github.com/mora-lab/benchmarks/blob/master/time-course/workflows/TcGSA/TcGSA.ipynb)

6. [PHANTOM](https://github.com/mora-lab/benchmarks/blob/master/time-course/workflows/Phantom/phantom.ipynb)

7. [FUNNEL-GSEA](https://github.com/mora-lab/benchmarks/blob/master/time-course/workflows/FUNNEL/FUNNEL.ipynb)
